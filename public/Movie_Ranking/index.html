Web Series Watchlist

Recent Additions to My Binge-Watching Adventure:

1.Suits

A thrilling saga of how good looks and a complete lack of legal qualifications can lead you to become a top-tier lawyer. An amazing bunch of impossibly attractive people in tailored suits navigate the legal world with little regard for actual rules or ethics. A remarkable drama where courtroom battles are won with witty one-liners and smug smirks.

2.The Recruit

An entertaining web series of clueless rookies navigating the world of espionage. It's fun watching aspiring spies fumble through secret missions, mistaking staplers for spy gadgets and excel sheets for classified documents.

3.The Young Royals

A royal version of high school drama, complete with crown-wearing teenagers and a royal cafeteria serving tea and scones.
